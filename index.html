About Me

I am a web developer living near Dallas, Texas. After twenty years working in and managing a pizza restaurant I decided to shift gears. I changed my passion for technology into a career as a developer. As a student at Thinkful I have been introduced to a wider community of coders. I finally feel like I'm home. I am available for front and back end work using HTML, CSS, JavaScript, JQuery, React, Node, and Express. I am always learning new languages as well.

Aside from coding I enjoy all things Science Fiction and Fantasy, from Star Trek to Dungeons & Dragons. I am sure my love of coding will soon intersect my hobbies and lead to some fun projects.
